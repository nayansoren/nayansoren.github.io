About

I am working as an Assistant Professor of Economics at Giridih College, Giridih (A Constituent Unit of Vinoba Bhave University, Hazaribag).

I am also a doctoral student in economics at the Department of Humanities and Social Sciences, IIT Delhi.

My research focuses on Development Economics, particularly at the intersection of agriculture, labour, and rural transformation.

My doctoral research examines agrarian change, structural marginalisation, and livelihood reproduction with special reference to Jharkhand.

Research Interests

  • Agrarian Political Economy
  • Development Economics
  • Labour Economics
  • Rural Transformation
  • Inequality and Regional Development
