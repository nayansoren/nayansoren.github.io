About

I am working as an Assistant Professor of Economics at Giridih College, Giridih , a constituent unit of Vinoba Bhave University, Hazaribag.

I am also a doctoral student in economics at IIT Delhi .

My research focuses on Development Economics, particularly agrarian change, labour markets, and structural transformation in India.

Research

My work lies at the intersection of development economics, agrarian political economy, and labour studies.

  • Agrarian Political Economy
  • Development Economics
  • Labour Economics
  • Rural Transformation
  • Inequality and Regional Development
